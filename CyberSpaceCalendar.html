CyberSpace

Calendar​

The calendar is one of the four major features of CyberSpace.
The calender is used not only for tracking days but also for tracking events and deadlines.
You can change the priority of the events​
Notifications could also be customized​.
It also helps remind people what classes they have next​.

Main Calendar

The Main Calendar is a general area for important dates​
The Main Calendar can be viewed in different ways, like day, week and month​.
Day View will show what classes will occur at what time of day. It also shows upcoming quizzes and homework due dates that day​.
Week View shows when homework is due that week, and upcoming quizzes that week. Holidays and Breaks will also be shown​.
Month view contains the same features as Week View, but obviously, it is shown on a monthly scale​​.​

Task Board

One part of the calendar is the Task Board​
Task Board is sorted into sections: Not done, doing, and done, where in each section, you can put assignments​.
Assignments and quizzes will be automatically added to the task board, as well as the assignment dates and due dates being put on the main calendar​.
Reminders will be given out at reasonable times (ex. 1 day, 1 hour, 15 min, 5 min before due date) or custom times.​
You can also add personal notes to the Task Board.​
